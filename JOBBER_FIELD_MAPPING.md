# Where Your Form Fields Show in Jobber

When an order syncs to Jobber, your "YOUR INFORMATION" form fields appear here:

---

## In Jobber: **Clients** → open the client (customer name)

| Your form field      | Where in Jobber | Notes |
|----------------------|-----------------|--------|
| **First Name***      | Client → **First name** | Top of client profile |
| **Last Name***       | Client → **Last name**  | Top of client profile |
| **Email Address***   | Client → **Emails**      | Contact section |
| **Phone***           | Client → **Phones**     | Contact section (primary number) |
| **Alternate Phone**  | Client → **Phones** (second number) | Same contact section as primary phone |
| **Address 1***        | Client → **Properties** → Property → **Address** → **Street 1** | Service address |
| **Address 2**        | Same property → **Street 2** (or Unit, etc.) | |
| **Zip Code***        | Same property → **Postal code** | |
| **City***             | Same property → **City** | |
| **State***            | Same property → **Province** | |

---

## In Jobber: **Jobs** → open the job linked to that order

| Your form field              | Where in Jobber | Notes |
|------------------------------|-----------------|--------|
| **Special Instructions or Requests** | Job → **Notes** | Appears as a line like: `Special Instructions: [what they wrote]` |

Job notes also include: services summary, zone/fee, total, deposit, duration, appointment day, etc.

---

## Quick summary

- **Client profile** in Jobber = name, email, phone, and **property address** (Address 1, 2, City, State, Zip).
- **Job** for that client = **Notes** section has Special Instructions plus other booking details.

To see a customer’s info: Jobber → **Clients** → click the client → check **Contact** and **Properties**.  
To see their instructions: **Jobs** → click the job → **Notes**.
